About Arcade-DM

A web app to help users and the government prepare for and reallocate resources for floods.

Floods are one of the most dangerous and frequent natural disasters in the world.

The Kerala floods of 2024 were a major environmental disaster, marked by severe monsoon rains that caused rivers to overflow and inundate large areas of the state. This catastrophic event led to the loss of numerous lives, displacement of thousands of residents, and extensive damage to infrastructure. Over 20,000 homes were destroyed, and critical facilities such as schools and hospitals were severely impacted. The economic damage was substantial, with losses running into billions of USD. In response, Kerala's government, along with national and international aid organizations, launched a comprehensive relief and recovery effort. This included rebuilding infrastructure, providing aid to affected families, and implementing measures to improve flood resilience. The 2024 floods highlighted the urgent need for advanced flood prediction technologies and enhanced disaster management strategies to better prepare for and respond to future extreme weather events.

To address this issue, our team developed Arcade-DM, a tool designed to predict floods and assess their impact before they occur. We present this information through an interactive graphical interface, making it both engaging and easy for individuals and governments to comprehend.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Inspiration

    According to the World Resources Institute, over 80% of India's population—approximately 1.08 billion people—are at risk of flooding. Each year, floods lead to economic losses of nearly 15 billion USD in India. For example, the recent floods in Kerala resulted in over 100 fatalities and the destruction of more than 15,000 homes and buildings. These floods occur almost annually in flood-prone regions, causing severe damage to lives and property. The main cause is the overflow of rivers, reservoirs, and other water bodies due to heavy monsoon rains. To mitigate this problem, we aim to create an effective flood prediction system for India, addressing the current gap caused by a lack of high-quality data for reliable predictions.

  • How we built it

  • - Developing the most comprehensive dataset for flood prediction in India.
    - Building a highly accurate ML model with over 98% precision.
    - Designing a user-friendly interface for the web app.
    - Generating detailed plots for effective data visualization.

Arcade-DM is a tool that leverages Machine Learning and Data Visualization to display real-time flood plots and heatmaps. It also provides predictions for future floods and assesses potential damage based on location.
